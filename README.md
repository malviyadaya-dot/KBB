# 🤑 Kaun Banega Bhikari (KBB)

Welcome to **Kaun Banega Bhikari** - the only game in the world where winning means going absolutely bankrupt! 

This is a fun, highly interactive, and ultimate troll parody of the famous TV show KBC. Instead of winning money, every correct answer adds a massive debt to your head, ranging from ₹1,000 all the way to ₹7,00,00,000! 

**Created by:** Sanket Malviya 👑

## 🔥 Danger Features
* **📉 Reverse Logic:** You give the right answer, you lose money!
* **🎤 Brutal Roasts:** Computer Ji won't spare you. Expect savage taunts for every move.
* **🔫 Cinematic Entry:** A 3D dropping entry stamp accompanied by a screen-shaking gunshot sound effect.
* **⏱️ Toxic Tik-Tiki Ji:** A 30-second timer that literally insults you if you take too long to think.
* **🎧 Meme Audio Integration:** Custom sound effects for correct answers, wrong answers, and the iconic "Arre bhai aap to piche he pad gaye yaar!" dialogue.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **HTML5** (Structure)
* **CSS3** (Animations, 3D Transforms, Responsive Mobile Layout)
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (Game Logic, DOM Manipulation, Custom Audio/TTS)
* *Zero external libraries used! Pure raw coding.*

## 🎮 How to Play
1. Enter the game and click "Hotseat Par Aaiye!"
2. Read the relatable, school/life-based funny questions.
3. Try to answer incorrectly to save your money. (But wait, can you?)
4. Survive the 30-second timer.
5. Get roasted, get broke.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
No real money is involved. This is purely for entertainment, laughs, and trolling your friends. Play at your own risk, *aur apne pocket money se karza chukane ke liye taiyar rahein!*
